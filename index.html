Movie of the month

The Martian

As a fan of both science fiction and science fact, I’ve been really appreciating the spate of new films that take place in space that don’t have to do with alien menaces or laser battles.

I love those, too, but we need more celebrations of what we could actually accomplish in the near future as well as what might happen or probably would never happen.

Sitting down for Ridley Scott’s The Martian, based on the novel by Andy Weir, all I could do was hold my breath and hope it would deliver on its potential; the ear-to-ear grin I had as the end credits rolled meant my hope wasn’t lost.
