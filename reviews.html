The Martian
9.5/10


During a mission to Mars, astronaut Mark Watney(Matt Damon) is presumed killed in action after a furious storm and is left behind by his crew.
Watney has survived and finds himself isolated,marooned and stranded on the other planet.With only a limited amount of supplies,he must use his wits to survive and to signal home.

Ridley Scott proves that he is still the master of science fiction by directing this new science fiction movie. There are gripping moments in this movie which will capture audiences attention.Mark Watney has to use intelligence as help is only 140 million miles away from home.
The Martian has an epic tag line "Bring him home".

This is a gripping,intelligent adaption from the book and an amazing performance from Matt Damon.


Spectre
7/10


MI6 secret service agent James Bond(Daniel Craig) recieves a cryptic message from his past which sends him on a trail to uncover a sinister organisation.

M(Ralph Fiennes) continues to battle political forces to keep the service active.As Bond gets closer to pursuing the organisation, he uncovers a shocking truth behind SPECTRE.

Spectre focuses on making Daniel Craigs rebooted Bond series to match the originals.However it is not as good as its previous movie(Skyfall).Spectre is the next chapter from skyfall which matchs up more with the originals.


Black Mass
7.5/10


This is the true story of Whitey Bulger(Johnny Depp),the brother of state senator(Benedict Cumberbatch) and head of the most ruthless criminal gang in South Bostom,whom later becomes an informant to bring down a rival Mafia family invading his turf. Johnny Depps performance is very compelling in this biographical crime story.

Black Mass tends to exaggerate from the actual fact based story, however audiences will be stunned with Johnny Depps best performance in years.It is amazing seeing Johnny Depp playing more adult roles.


Jurassic world
7.5/10


A new theme park has been constructed on the site of where Jurassic park used to exist.Everything seems to be flawless until a giant experimented killing machine escapes from a containment area and goes on a killing spree.

Jurassic world is fast pacing, however it doesnt quite match the originals but it will provide lots of entertainment for fans and newcomers to the series.


Mad Max:Fury Road
9/10


A woman(Charlize Theron) forms a rebellion against a tyrant in post-apocalyptic Australia in pursuit of her home with an alliance of female prisoners and a lone drifter named Max(Tom Hardy).

Mad Max:Fury Road resurects the old series with amazing action sequences and very effective narration and visual effect.The original fans will be more than just satisfied with the new Mad Max franchise.
